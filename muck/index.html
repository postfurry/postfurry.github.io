Postfurry MUCK

Postfurry MUCK is a text-based server for roleplay, socializing, and kink. Previously known as 12Fold MUCK, the server has been running since 2005 and offers plenty of different spaces to explore and interact. The sights you can see include:

  • A theme-park solar system where each planet is a different kink playground
  • The diverse and disjointied districts of a posthuman megastructure
  • A sprawling city where it's always night and dream-logic holds sway
  • … and whatever else you can imagine, if you'd like to build it!

We welcome any new players who are interested in respectfully contributing to the vibe, whether you're new to this sort of RP or not. We're always happy to see new faces, and get folks up to speed with how MUCKs work, including extensive help documents on this site.
